Finding Limit

\begin{align*}
&\lim_{x \to h}\frac{f(x + h) - f(x)}{x+h-x}\\
&=\lim_{x \to h}\frac{(x+h)^{2} - x^{2}}{h}\\
&=\lim_{x \to h}\frac{x^{2} + 2xh + h^{2} - x^{2}}{h}\\
&=\lim_{x \to h}2x + h\\
&\text{As }x\text{ approaches }h \text{, h approaches }0\\
&\therefore \lim_{x \to h}2x + h = \lim_{h \to 0}2x + h\\
&=2x
\end{align*}
